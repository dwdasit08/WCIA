Awareness Campaign

#NoticeKaroIndia

A campaign to make disability visible and demand equal rights, accessibility, and respect.

Why #NoticeKaroIndia?

Despite progress, persons with disabilities often remain invisible in mainstream discourse. #NoticeKaroIndia aims to:

  • Raise awareness about the rights of persons with disabilities.
  • Advocate for accessible infrastructure, education, and employment.
  • Celebrate the achievements of Divyangjan in sports, arts, and society.

Join the campaign by sharing your story, using the hashtag, and supporting WCIA's initiatives.
