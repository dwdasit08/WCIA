5TH NATIONAL WHEELCHAIR CRICKET CHAMPIONSHIP 2026

Uttar Pradesh — Crowned Champions

Uttar Pradesh emerged as the champions of the 5th National Wheelchair Cricket Championship 2026 after a thrilling Mega Finale against Haryana. Displaying exceptional teamwork, determination, and fighting spirit, Uttar Pradesh secured a memorable 5-run victory to lift the prestigious title.

The championship witnessed outstanding performances from players across the country and highlighted the spirit of resilience, inclusion, and excellence in Wheelchair Cricket. The tournament concluded on a grand note, celebrating the growing movement of adaptive sports in India.

Our Programs

Driving change through focused initiatives that empower lives.

Breaking Barriers

Organizing domestic and international wheelchair cricket tournaments to provide a competitive platform and promote national pride through inclusive sports.

Wheels of Freedom

Providing high-performance sports wheelchairs to promising cricketers across India to enhance performance and ensure participation in national and international competitions.

Join Us

National Training Camp

Identifying, training, and nurturing talented PwDs in wheelchair cricket through structured national-level camps focusing on skill development, fitness, and team building.

Join Us

Accessible Rehabilitation Centers

Empowering differently abled individuals through focused rehabilitation programs that restore confidence, mobility, and purpose through physical therapy and community reintegration.

Join Us

Sports Center, Lucknow

A dedicated rehabilitation center under Sewa Niketan providing accessible physiotherapy, psychological support, and vocational counseling for persons with disabilities.

Disability Sports Training Centre
