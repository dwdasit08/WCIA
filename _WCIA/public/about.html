About WCIA

About Us

Wheelchair Cricket India Association (WCIA) is a non‑profit organization registered under Section 80G, dedicated to promoting wheelchair cricket across India.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower persons with disabilities through the sport of wheelchair cricket, providing a platform for talent, inclusion, and national pride.

Vision

To make wheelchair cricket a mainstream sport in India, with world‑class infrastructure, training, and international recognition.

Our Journey

Founded in 2018, WCIA has grown from a small group of enthusiasts to a national movement. We have organized 5 national championships, toured internationally, and built a community of over 1000 players across 20+ states. Our efforts have been recognised by the Government of India and international cricketing bodies.
