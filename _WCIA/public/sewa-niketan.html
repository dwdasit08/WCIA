Sewa Niketan

Dedicated to providing accessible physiotherapy, psychological support, and vocational counseling for persons with disabilities.

Our Facilities

  • Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation
  • Psychological Counseling
  • Vocational Training and Skill Development
  • Sports Wheelchair Fitting and Maintenance
  • Community Integration Programs

Located in Lucknow, Sewa Niketan serves as a hub for empowerment and inclusion, impacting hundreds of lives annually.
