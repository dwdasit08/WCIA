Historic Journey

Ladakh Expedition

From Delhi to Ladakh – a journey of courage, determination, and awareness for disability rights.

The Expedition

In March 2026, a team of Divyangjan (persons with disabilities) embarked on a historic expedition from Delhi to Ladakh, covering over 1000 kilometers. The journey aimed to raise awareness about the capabilities of persons with disabilities and promote inclusive tourism and infrastructure.

The expedition was supported by WCIA, the Government of India, and various NGOs. It received widespread media coverage and inspired countless individuals.
