Drive With Ability

Empowering persons with disabilities through mobility, sports, and community support.

About the Campaign

Drive With Ability is a movement to promote independent mobility for persons with disabilities. It includes:

  • Distribution of sports wheelchairs to aspiring cricketers.
  • Awareness drives on accessible infrastructure.
  • Training programs for wheelchair handling and sports skills.

Join us in making mobility a reality for all.
